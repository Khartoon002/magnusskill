Magnus Platform · Quick FAQs

These are some of the common questions people ask before joining Magnus. Use them as a guide — and still chat a verified PayFac on WhatsApp if you need more clarity.

Is Magnus paying or is it a scam? +
Magnus Platform pays users who follow the correct registration process, pay to a verified account and actively use the platform. This dashboard helps you avoid fake links and random accounts by guiding you to a verified PayFac.
How do I register on Magnus? +
Click Open My Dashboard, fill your details, select your entry plan, then pay to the verified account shown on your screen. Afterwards, use the Submit Proof button to send your evidence via Telegram, Email or WhatsApp for activation.
Can I use OPay to pay for Magnus? +
Yes, if you choose the OPay Bank option on the payment method screen. The system will show you a special account dedicated to OPay users. You can also choose Other Banks if you prefer normal bank transfer.
What happens after I pay? +
Once you pay, click Submit Payment Proof on this dashboard. Choose Telegram, Email or WhatsApp, attach your screenshot, and send. A verified PayFac will confirm your payment and complete your Magnus registration.
